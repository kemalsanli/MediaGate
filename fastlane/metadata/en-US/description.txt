MediaGate converts unsupported media files and saves them directly to your Photos library. Share from any app — Files, Safari, Mail, Messages — and MediaGate handles the rest.

No cloud uploads. No accounts. No tracking. Everything happens on your device.

▶ HOW IT WORKS
Tap the share button on any file, select "Save with MediaGate", and the converted file appears in Photos within seconds.

▶ SUPPORTED FORMATS

Video → MP4
MKV, AVI, WMV, WebM, FLV, MPEG, 3GP, TS, VOB, OGV, RM, DIVX, and more.

Image → PNG / JPEG
WebP, BMP, TIFF, SVG, PSD, TGA, RAW (CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG, ORF, RW2, RAF, PEF, and more).

Passthrough (save directly)
MP4, MOV, HEVC, JPG, PNG, HEIF, HEIC, GIF, AVIF.

58 file extensions across 3 categories.

▶ KEY FEATURES

• Hardware-accelerated encoding via VideoToolbox
• Works from the share sheet — no need to open the app
• Batch conversion — share up to 20 files at once
• Adjustable image quality and video quality presets
• Preserve or strip metadata and location data
• Compress compatible files to reduce size
• 36 languages with in-app language switching
• Zero network requests — fully offline
• No ads, no subscription

▶ PRIVACY
MediaGate uses add-only photo library access. It cannot read your existing photos. No analytics, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.

▶ OPEN SOURCE
MediaGate is free and open source under GPLv3. View the source code on GitHub.
