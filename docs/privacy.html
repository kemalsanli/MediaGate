MediaGate

Privacy Policy

April 12, 2026

MediaGate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No data collection

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No user accounts
  • No network requests beyond App Store receipt validation
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No crash reporting services

Media files

All conversion happens entirely on your device. Shared files are temporarily stored in the app sandbox, saved to your Photos library after conversion, then deleted from temporary storage.

Photo library

MediaGate requests add-only access to your photo library. It saves converted files. It cannot read or browse your existing photos.

In-app purchases

Optional tips are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. MediaGate receives no payment information.

Third-party services

None. The only external dependency — FFmpeg — runs entirely on-device.
